# Samantha Key Marketing Context

## App Overview

- **App Name:** Samantha Key
- **Bundle ID:** com.alexmitre.samanthakey
- **Category:** Productivity
- **Platform:** iOS
- **Price Model:** 3-day free trial, then MXN $149/month
- **Launch Date:** Not yet launched
- **Current Version:** 1.0.0

## Value Proposition

- **Problem:** People need to reply in another language without copying text into a translator app.
- **Target Audience:** Travelers, international workers, students, creators, and anyone messaging across languages.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Voice-first keyboard flow with real-time OpenAI translation and no saved audio or transcript history.
- **Elevator Pitch:** Samantha Key lets you speak once and insert translated text anywhere you can type.

## Competitors

| App | Strengths | Weaknesses |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TypeLess | Strong AI keyboard positioning | Less explicit voice translation privacy story |
| VoiceKey | Direct voice keyboard promise | Hard to differentiate without stronger translation workflow |
| Google Translate | Broad language awareness | Requires switching context and copying/pasting |
| Apple Translate | Native and trusted | Not positioned as a cross-app keyboard |

## Goals

1. Ship a polished v1 suitable for App Store review.
2. Validate subscription conversion for a monthly utility price.
3. Build reusable OpenAI Realtime keyboard infrastructure for future language products.

## Markets

- **Primary:** Mexico, United States
- **Secondary:** Europe, Brazil, Japan, Korea
- **Languages:** English, Spanish, French, Italian, Korean, Portuguese, Simplified Chinese, Japanese
